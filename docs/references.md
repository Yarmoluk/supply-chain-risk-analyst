# References

## ISO Standards and Official Documents

1. **ISO 28000:2022 - Security and resilience — Security management systems — Requirements**
   - International Organization for Standardization
   - https://www.iso.org/standard/79612.html
   - Official standard specifying requirements for security management systems in supply chains

2. **ISO 31000:2018 - Risk management — Guidelines**
   - International Organization for Standardization
   - https://www.iso.org/standard/65694.html
   - General risk management principles and guidelines applicable across all contexts

3. **ISO 9001:2015 - Quality management systems — Requirements**
   - International Organization for Standardization
   - https://www.iso.org/standard/62085.html
   - Quality management standard that integrates with ISO 28000
